# Rock Paper Scissors Lizard Spock

An enhanced version of the classic Rock Paper Scissors game, featuring five gestures instead of three. This variation was popularized by the TV show *The Big Bang Theory* and reduces the probability of ties while adding strategic depth.

### How to Play

1. Select one of the five gestures: Rock, Paper, Scissors, Lizard, or Spock
2. The computer randomly selects its gesture
3. The winner is determined by the rules above
4. Score is tracked across multiple rounds
5. Try to outsmart the computer and achieve the highest score!
